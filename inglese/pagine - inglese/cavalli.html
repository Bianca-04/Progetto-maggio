ARABIAN

From the sheen of his coat to his iconic snub head, the sight of an Arabian
leaves everyone in awe. It is one of the most renowned and recognizable breeds in the world,
with a distinctive past and origins.

FRIESIAN

It's hard not to be amazed watching a Friesian gallop, with its tail and mane fluttering in the wind.
If you are looking for a companion with the courage
a knight and the elegance of a queen,
the Friesian is the horse right for you.

AKHAL-TEKE

Known as the "heavenly horse".
With a slender body reminiscent of a cheetah, a coat that sometimes has metallic undertones, and a lineage that goes back over 3,000 years, the Akhal Teke is
one of the equine breeds most exotic and loved
in the world.

WILD HORSE

Jorvik's wild horses are a very ancient breed. They are not easily tamed. They are the ones who choose their jockey. Bonding with a Jorvik Wild Horse is a bond of mutual trust.

ANDALUSIAN

The compact build and agile
and elevated movements make the Andalusian the perfect horse for riding. It is a versatile
horse that adapts to all disciplines, but has a natural talent for dressage.
